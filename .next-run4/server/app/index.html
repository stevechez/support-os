Questions, answered

Which AI models can I use?

SupportOS works with leading AI providers like Claude, GPT, and Gemini. Connect your own API keys, choose the models that fit your workflow, and pay providers directly with no AI markup from SupportOS.

How does SupportOS keep AI responses accurate?

Connect your documentation, files, and URLs to your knowledge base. SupportOS grounds responses in your actual business information and provides citations so your team can trust the answers.

Can AI reply without human supervision?

Only when you choose. SupportOS can draft replies for review or automatically resolve specific low-risk workflows that your team defines.

Is our business data secure?

Yes. Each workspace is isolated with enterprise-grade security controls, including row-level security, protected authentication, and complete audit history for administrative actions.
